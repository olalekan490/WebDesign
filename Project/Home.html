Welcome to Food and Us the idea of providing home away from home where an average Nigerian can enjoy quality and comfort at the lowest price possible was the foundation that gave birth to Food and Us, in 1994, from the guardhouse in Ilupeju, the dream was born and has spread to more than 25 locations across the country. From a dream it has grown to become a corporate brand and a standard in the confectionery industry in Nigeria offering comfort and wellness to individuals, groups and corporate organizations.In 1996 a major milestone was recorded when African delicacies were incorporated in the list of menu served on the floor of Food and Us Restaurants, a feat that has become common place among other Quick Service Restaurants.
